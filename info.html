The Club

Clarkstown South High School, along side the Assets class, has its own club. While the class focuses on utilizing the 40 developmental assets, the club is a way to truly show those assets outside of a classroom. The club focuses more on events to help benefit those in and out of our community.

We meet every other Thursday in room 203 from 2:10 to 2:30 pm.

The Class

Clarkstown South High School is proud to be one of the only high schools to offer a semester elective entitled “Assets Get Active.” The strength-based course challenges students to fully learn, experience, and utilize the 40 developmental Assets. The curriculum includes activities, school and community events, journal writing, discussion, guest speakers, and independent projects.

To signup for the class see Ms. Solar or your guidance counselor.

For more information the class see or contact Ms. Solar (ssolar@cssd.edu)
